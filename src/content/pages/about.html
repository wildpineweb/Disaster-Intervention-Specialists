Anna Jones

Anna is a disaster relief and pack out specialist dedicated to helping families and property owners recover after unexpected loss. With a calm, organized approach and a deep knowledge of inventory management, content handling, and restoration processes, Anna ensures every item is treated with care and every client feels supported. She is known for her compassion, attention to detail, and ability to bring order to chaos—guiding clients through some of their most difficult moments with professionalism and empathy.

Anna's life away from work keeps her very busy as she enjoys spending time with her family. She also loves to travel, go wine tasting and soak up the sunshine and beauty of the outdoors. Being from the south, she enjoys cooking and hosts family dinners on Sunday evenings so that her children and grandchildren can have focused family time with her each week.

Jason Cockreham

As a Disaster Intervention Specialist, Jason Cockreham is on the front lines of helping families and businesses regain stability after unexpected events. His steady presence, industry knowledge, and client-first approach ensure every situation is handled with urgency, integrity, and respect. Jason believes in restoring not just property—but peace of mind.

Jason's family is his passion. He married his beautiful wife Kelly in 1992, and together they have three children and four grandchildren. If he isn't at work he is always doing something with his family. He loves spending time with the kids but also loves to hook up his trailer and sneak away with his wife, traveling the northwest.
